Happy New Year 
from YPT SF Bay

Dear YPT Bay Area members and friends,

Happy New Year! The new YPT Board is hard at work making plans to advance professional development and networking for our members in the year ahead. We would love to hear how we can best serve you in 2022 — please feel welcome to share your ideas with us at yptsfbay@gmail.com.

Kick off 2022 by becoming a YPT SF Bay member or renewing your membership. Membership costs just $20. Sign up as a new member (choose “San Francisco” as your chapter) or renew your membership. Tip: Check with your employer to see if they will cover your membership as part of your professional development.

Have any young professionals recently joined your community? Consider forwarding this email to them so they can get involved!

Could This Be You? Vice Chair for Programs

Are you interested in finding creative ways to engage our members? Help advance professional development and networking for YPT SF Bay members as our Programs Chair! The Vice Chair for Programs leads planning and execution for events, including registering attendees using Eventbrite and coordinating with guests and venues. Past events have included panel discussions, webinars, happy hours, paint nights, and other in-person or virtual activities, with attendances ranging from a dozen to hundreds. Board membership is a great way to build your leadership skills and grow your professional network.

Apply to join our board as Vice Chair for Programs. Email the YPT Board (yptsfbay@gmail.com) if you are interested. We’d be happy to set up a call to share more about the role and learn about your interest in joining the board.

Jan 30 – TransitCon

Date: Sunday, January 30, 2022
Time: 7:30am to 3:00pm
Location: Virtual, Register at transitcon.org

YPT SF Bay is proud to support TransitCon, a FREE and virtual transportation conference to be held on Sunday, January 30th. TransitCon seeks to democratize access to transportation information and networking by maintaining a free platform and encouraging students and young professionals to speak, elevating new voices and thinkers instead of relying on the same handful of industry leaders at each event. TransitCon will feature both sessions and workshops that will invite you to get involved and learn more about transit the world over. Register to attend and check out the speaker list (featuring Bay Area speakers John Bauters, Warren Wells, and Seung Lee) at transitcon.org
